If you are frequently watching some of your favorite videos on YouTube then YouTube Downloader HD is a must have software for you. YouTube Downloader HD is a free software available for Windows and Mac, that allows you to download youtube video in FLV or MP4 formats in HD quality.

Downloading your favorite YouTube videos locally will save you a sizable amount of internet bandwidth as well as you don't need to wait for streaming the video when your internet connection is little bit slow. You can download the software from its official website.
