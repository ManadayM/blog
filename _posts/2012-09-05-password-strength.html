The most common passwords in the world

Continuous advancements in internet technologies have blessed us with numerous internet services to ease up our life. Internet banking, online shopping, online entertainment and communication services are widely used nowadays. Having account with such services require us to create a username and password to access the specific service anytime, across the globe.

Password is crucial element to play securely over the internet. Today hackers and malwares have highly upgraded their skills to crack your personal identity to do the malicious activity from/with your account. So it's always advisable to have good password that could not be break easily. If you have ever thought, to know the strength of your password then "HowSecureIsMyPassword" is a sure shot for you.

It is a free password strength checker tool to calculate how long it would take for an average computer to crack your password using Brtue Force method. The longer duration, the better password. The site is quite simple to use: enter your password in the password box on the homepage, and your password's strength will be calculated.

"HowSecureIsMyPassword" runs within your browser and it's core calculation logic is based on JavaScript and so your password won't be transfered over the internet while you are checking the strength.

Check how secure your password is @ http://howsecureismypassword.net/
