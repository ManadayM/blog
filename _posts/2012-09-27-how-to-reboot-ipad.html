One of the best troubleshooting option till the date is to rebooting the device. iPad is no different. It could be possible that your iPad stops to responding your taps and touch gestures. You won't be able to exit from the app by pressing the Home Button. At such times, you are left with only one option, Reboot! And, that's why one should know how to reboot the iPad, incase it freezes up.

Upon restarting the iPad, it will close all the applications that were running in the background state. It will free up the memory hog caused by the buggy app.

Here's how to reboot your iPad:

How to Reboot Your iPad
  • Press the Power Button and Home Button simultaneously for 10 seconds. This will force the iPad to reboot.
  • Keep holding the two keys until your iPad screen goes black. You will see the white Apple logo on the screen once it gets restarted.
That's it. This kind of situation is rarely possible. However, you should be aware for what to do when your iPad stops responding.
