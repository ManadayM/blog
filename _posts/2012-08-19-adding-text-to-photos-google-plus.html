A simple image can turn into a funny image, if one can add some words/text on image before sharing with your Google plus circles. Of-course you can use some image processing software to do the same, but it is tedious and time consuming.

Google+ continuously adding new features to give better social network experience. Lately, Google+ introduced a feature to add big, bold text on your photos before sharing with your circles.




You can try it out, by adding a photo to your sharebox in Google+, then click Add Text button underneath the photo. You will be presented with a picture on right side and 3 textbox on left side to add text on picture.

The 3 textbox allows you to add text at top, middle and bottom of the image. After adding text click Save button.

That's all, enjoy.
