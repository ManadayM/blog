Apple may soon join to the mini tablets war with iPad Mini

The war of mini tablet could get tougher upon releasing of iPad mini. 

According to bloomberg, the rumored iPad mini will be launched in this October with a 7.85 inch display. The new smaller displays will be manufactured by both LG Display Co. and AU Optronics Corp.

TPK Holding Co. and Yeh Cheng Technology, a subsidiary of Foxconn group, will supply the lamination coating for the device.

Apple targeting to hold the mini tablet market share with the upcoming/existing faces of mini tablets like Microsoft Surface or Samsung Galaxy Tab 2 by offering the iPad mini. Still, there is no clear indication of what will be the resolution for the iPad mini's display.

Apple is planning a press event on September 12th to announce the rumored iPhone 5, and if Bloomberg's prediction of launching iPad mini in October is true, then we may hear something about the iPad mini at the same time.

This will be a good move as well as competition by Apple in the tablets technology. The iPad Mini has to compete especially with the newly released phablet Samsung Galaxy Note II and Samsung Galaxy Tab 2 tablet.
