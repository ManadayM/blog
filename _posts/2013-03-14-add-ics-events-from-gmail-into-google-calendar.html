Gmail team has released a very cool feature, today. Now, when you receive an attachment with calendar events in .ics format, you can add these events to your Google Calendar right from you Gmail Inbox with a single mouse click! This cool feature will save you much time from downloading and importing them manually to your calendar.


Additionally, the added event in calendar will show you a link back to the original email! So, you can easily find the extra details, if any, mentioned in the email. Check the below animated image, if you are not clear.
