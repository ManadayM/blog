In this post, lets look at the concept called Repin

Repin something - Reshare something you liked on your Pin Board
  • Using search for the things you like, you will find some results. You can click on Repin.
  • It will ask you where to Pin this article/thing i.e. in which Pin Board. specify that, and give it a suitable name and you are done.
