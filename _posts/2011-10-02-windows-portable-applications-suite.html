Portable Application (also known as Portable Apps) is a light weight computer software, that can be installed on removable devices like pen drive, memory card, USB Hard disks, etc. Programs in this category are typically 'lite' versions of their parent software.

You can carry all your favorite software, bookmarks, settings, emails and many more on your removable devices. PortableApps.com is popular portable software solution that allows you to take all your favorite software with you. 

Follow the instructions to run your favorite software right from your removable media.
Go to http://portableapps.com/suite and download the platform only version
Locate the downloaded setup file and double click on it to start installing
Initially, you will be asked to select your preferred language, accept the licence agreement. Accept the licence agreement and click Next button.

Insert removable media and browse it as destination folder then Click Install
That's it. Click Finish button, PortableApps menu will be popup automatically, from where you can manage applications, browse documents on your removable media, and much more.
