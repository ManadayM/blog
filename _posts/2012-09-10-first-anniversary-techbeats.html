Message from the admin; Straight from the heart

A year ago, I was asked to start a technology blog to spread the knowledge to utilize the features available in softwares and in internet services - which would be of ease so that any layman can understand the process to utilize softwares as well as online services to make things more simpler.

I started Tech Beats on 10th September, 2011 with a mission to spread knowledge about Tools and Technologies available to all of us. During the year, I published almost 51 posts (40+ posts in draft!) ranging from How-Tos posts to Tips and tricks.
