You know what, if you are using Windows 7, then you should be happy. You know the reason why? Ok lets get a bit of information on it!

Windows 7 the successor of Windows Vista is already designed and engineered to be much faster than that of Vista.

But, obviously if you are using some heavy tools, say Photoshop, or Flash or some development tools like eclipse and visual studio, then its better that you have good amount of hardware resources at its disposal, to avoid performance issues.
