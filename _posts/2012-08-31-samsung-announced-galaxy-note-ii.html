Samsung announced a new version of Galaxy Note as well as the Android Powered Digital Point-and-Shoot Camera at an electronic trade show - IFA, Berlin. The new Galaxy Note II gives unique user experience in content creation.
Samsung Announced Galaxy Note II - The most powerful smartphone sparks everyone’s creative inspirations
It has AMOLED touchscreen with 5.5 inch display and 1280 x 720 pixels density. It comes with a high performance 1.6 GHz quad-core processor with 2 GB RAM!

This new version will be available in three different versions by its capacity - 16 GB, 32 GB and 64 GB with 4G enabled. The cool feature about this version it its stylus which is designed to ease up drawing and writing for those who are not too much used to of touchscreens.


It has an 8 megapixels camera with HD video recording and playback and a 1.9 megapixels camera on front.

The new Galaxy Note II comes with the Air View, which allows users to hover with the S Pen over an image gallery, email or video to preview content without having to open it.

The Galaxy Note II is powered by Android™ 4.1, Jelly Bean. So, users who've been waiting for the Android's latest version can get this one.

According to Samsung, the Galaxy Note II will be available in Asia and Europe from this October. But, the price has not been revealed yet.

  • Operating System - Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean)
  • Display - 5.5” HD Super AMOLED (1,280 x 720)
  • Camera -

    • Sub(Front): 1.9 Megapixel VT Camera
  • S Pen optimized features -
    • S Pen Experience 
      • S Note, S Planner, Email with hand-writing integration
      • S Pen Keeper
      • Quick Command, Easy Clip, Photo Note, Paper Artist
    • Air View
    • Shape Match, Formula Match, Idea Visualizer
  • Memory -
    • 16/32/64GB User memory ＋ 2GB (RAM)
    • MicroSD (up to 64 GB)
  • Google Mobile Services -
    • Google Search, Google Maps, Gmail, Google Latitude
    • Google Play Store, Google Plus, YouTube, Google Talk
    • Google Places, Google Navigation, Google Downloads, Google Chrome browser
    • Google Play Books, Google Play Movies
